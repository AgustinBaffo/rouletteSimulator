◆ updateBetList()

void Player::updateBetList ( bool  hasWon)
private

Update betList based on the result of the previous bet. If the player wins, he add the amount bet to the bottom of the list. If the player loses, he crosses out the two extreme numbers and the list gets smaller. In the next round the procedure is repeated. For example, if the player has the list [1,2,3,4] he will bet 5 (1+4=5). So:

  • If he wins, he add 5 to the list ([1,2,3,4,5]) and then he will bet 6 (1+5=6).
  • If he loses, he removes extremes from the list ([2,3]) and then he will bet 5 (2+3=5).

If the list runs out of numbers, the player also resets the list.
